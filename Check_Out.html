Our newest features give you even more control over your files and folders, and make collaboration even easier.

View-only permissions for shared folders

Shared folders let you give team members immediate access to your most recent work, but you may not necessarily want them to be able to change things around. With view-only permissions for shared folders, you can make sure no one accidentally deletes an important file.

Check out the Collaborate with shared folders article to learn more.

Set passwords and expirations for shared links

Sending files to clients, vendors, and team members shouldn't be a headache. With passwords and expiration dates now available for shared links, you can rest assured that your Q3 financials, proprietary design work, and top-secret launch calendar all remain on lock-down.

Check out the Send Links to your work article to learn more.
