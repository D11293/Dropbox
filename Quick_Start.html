Quick start: sync and share

Only have a few minutes? Perfect!

If you're in a hurry, make sure you check out these two essential sections so you can hit the ground running.

Set up and sync your devices

To get the most out of Dropbox, make sure you install the Dropbox application on all your computers and mobile devices. Then, move all your works files into the Dropbox folder. Once you do, you'll always have access to your files no matter where you are.

Check out the Set up category to start syncing!

Become a sharing master

Dropbox has two ways to share files with others: shared folders and shared links. Shared folders are great for collaborating on projects, while shared links are helpful for sending copies of your files to teammates and clients.

Check out the Share category to make sure you know how to do both!
