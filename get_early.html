Get early access

Dropbox keeps getting better. Business customers get priority access to new features, so make sure to opt in to our early access program.

We're always working to make Dropbox even better, and we need your help to do it.

Admins can choose to get early access to our most cutting-edge features before we release them to larger audiences. You'll also have the chance to send us feedback on how new features can be improved.

Getting early access

  1. Sign in to your admin account.
  2. Click on Admin Console in the left sidebar.
  3. Select the Account tab on the left sidebar.
  4. Click the Early Access tab, and then select "Get early access."
